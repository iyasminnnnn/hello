Scratch - Packman

Subject: Computing

Age range: 7-11

Resource type: Lesson (complete)

Learner worksheets providing instructions at 5 different levels (1-4 & PScale) to support the development of the game PACMAN, from very simple (to support moderate learning difficulties) to Level 4 (including more complex number skills).

I wanted all students to feel they had independently solved the problems they were faced with in my lessons and to feel they had created a game without help. So when Rosie (SEN MLD), completed the PScale sheet independently, she was so proud, more so because she had been able to help others out. Charlie had been really successful with the Level 3 sheet in PONG so I tried the Level 4 sheet here (but with the hint cards). This time he was really challenged.

This lesson is best if it follows the skill building Crazy Collisions lesson and PONG and FOGGER.

Opportunity for Creativity
Once the game was up and running students added additional levels, new monsters, challenges and treasure to collect. I normally insisted on their development of the basic game first as I used this as an assessment of skill level activity.
