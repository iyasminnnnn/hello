Ecommerce site for selling of vynil disks

Vynil disks are analog sound storage mediums, flat plastic (polyvinyl chloride) circles with a spiral groove holding audio information, played on a turntable where a needle reads the groove's vibrations, converting them to sound; known for their warm, unique sound, common formats include 12-inch LPs (33 1/3 rpm) for albums and 7-inch 45s for singles, offering a tactile, lossless listening experience popular for their aesthetic and connection to original recordings.

Vinyl discs are primarily made from Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC), a strong, flexible plastic, mixed with various additives like polyvinyl acetate (PVA)
